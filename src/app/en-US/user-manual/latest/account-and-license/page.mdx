# Account and License

## Account Registration

Please click<Link href="https://cc.chitubox.com/register"> here </Link>and sign up if you have not registered a CHITUBOX account.

## License Key

An account can have multiple license keys. The license key will be automatically linked to your account when you make a purchase. This license key cannot be unbound from the account.

<Admonition type="danger" title="Warning">
	Please do not share your license key with anybody and bind it to your
	account right after you receive it.
</Admonition>

<Admonition type="note" title="Attention">
	CHITUBOX Dental uses online account verification. Please ensure that your
	computer is connected to the internet.
</Admonition>

### Activate and Bind License Keys

1. A pop-up window will appear when CHITUBOX Dental starts up for the first time.

    <ImageContainer
    	src={
    		"/images/docs/en-US/user-manual/1.x.x/CHITUBOX-Dental-License01.png"
    	}
    />

    <Admonition type="note" title="Attention">
    	A license check shall starts after user login. The
    	<b>Start Program</b>
    	dialog will not appear if the account does not meet the requirements
    	(the account or the computer is already in use)
    </Admonition>

1. Enter your license key:

    <ImageContainer
    	src={
    		"/images/docs/en-US/user-manual/1.x.x/CHITUBOX-Dental-License02.png"
    	}
    />

    Clicking on the **Confirm** button activates the license key and binds it to your computer. The effective period of the key starts from the day the key is activated.

### Unbind License Keys

You will need to unbind your license key from your current computer if you want to migrate CHITUBOX Dental to a new machine.

Log into your <Link href="https://cc.chitubox.com/login">CHITUBOX account</Link> on web.

Click on **My Products** > **My License Keys**

<ImageContainer
	src={
		"/images/docs/en-US/user-manual/1.x.x/CHITUBOX-Dental-License03.png"
	}
/>

Click on **Managed Linked PC** > **Unlink**

<ImageContainer
	src={
		"/images/docs/en-US/user-manual/1.x.x/CHITUBOX-Dental-License04.png"
	}
/>

Your license key will be unbound from your current computer, and then you can re-activate the key on a new machine.
